RtI/MTI Presentation

I developed and presented these slides (and related training materials) to the teachers at an elementary school where I was just assigned the principalship. The school was in the second year of an RtI project grant, but was failing to meet the recommendations and conditions of that grant.

This presentation was the kickoff to the training teachers needed to help them not just comply with the grant, but to understand and be fully on board with its goals for the betterment of our students.
